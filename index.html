Ogma

Beautifully simple logging

Get Started

Easy to Use

Ogma was made to be a super simple logger that can integrate easily with your Node application.

Colors and JSON Support

Ogma has the ability to break up your normal logs into easy to read logs for both humans and machines with color and JSON support based on configs.

High Speeds

Ogma boasts some pretty impressive speed, keeping up with well known loggers like Pino without a problem.
